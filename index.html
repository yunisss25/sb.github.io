What is Starbucks?

Starbucks, founded in Seattle in 1971, is a global coffeehouse chain known for its premium coffee and welcoming atmosphere. With thousands of stores in over 80 countries, it emphasizes sustainability, ethical sourcing, and community impact.

What do they offer?

Starbucks offers coffee, tea, seasonal drinks, pastries, and sandwiches, along with retail products like coffee beans and mugs. Customers can personalize orders and use the mobile app for rewards and pre-ordering, all while supporting sustainability efforts like ethical sourcing and waste reduction.

Where can you find it?

Starbucks is conveniently located in malls, office buildings, and airports, making it easy to grab your favorite coffee on the go. With their mobile app, you can order ahead, earn rewards, and skip the line, so you can enjoy a refreshing drink anytime, anywhere. Visit Starbucks today and treat yourself to a moment of comfort!

COFFEE

Starbucks offers a wide range of coffee beverages, from bold espresso drinks like Lattes and Cappuccinos to freshly brewed options like Pike Place Roast and refreshing Cold Brews. Whether you prefer a hot cup to start your day or a chilled Frappuccino for a sweet treat, Starbucks has something for every coffee lover.

PASTRY

Starbucks serves a variety of delicious pastries, including buttery Croissants, sweet Muffins, and indulgent Cakes like Lemon and Carrot Cake. Perfectly paired with a coffee, their pastries offer a satisfying bite, whether you're enjoying a morning snack or an afternoon pick-me-up.
